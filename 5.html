Roadmap/Tutorial of how to learn programming

Step 1:

Open youtube, freecodecamp, Udemy,etc.

Step 2:

Search for your desired tech stack or language.

Step 3:

If you are using Udemy buy a course you like or if you are using YouTube or FreeCodeCamp you can get courses for free.

Step 4:

Last and most important step practice your skills using Leetcode or similar app
